# Project charter

## Project title
Profitability Thresholds in Indian Quick Commerce

## Research question
What operational conditions are required for an Indian quick-commerce dark 
store to achieve sustainable EBITDA profitability — and how do these differ 
between a Tier-1 and Tier-2 city?

## Objective
Develop a quantitative unit economics model for a representative dark store 
(Blinkit, Zepto, Swiggy Instamart) that identifies the exact thresholds of 
order volume, SKU mix, and logistics efficiency required for profitability.

## Deliverables
- Executive slide deck (1 page)
- Detailed technical appendix
- Python-based profitability model
- Sensitivity analysis
- Tier-1 vs Tier-2 comparison

## Success criteria
- All assumptions sourced and documented
- Reproducible calculations
- Clear recommendation supported by data

## Models planned
1. Unit economics P&L waterfall
2. Poisson demand modeling
3. Sensitivity and correlation analysis

## Out of scope
- Primary data collection
- Platform-level P&L
- International markets
